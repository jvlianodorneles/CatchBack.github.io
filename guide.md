# CATCHBACK - COMPLETE USER GUIDE

**CatchBack** is a background video and audio capture application that acts as a "black box" for your daily life. It continuously monitors and allows you to save the moments that just happened with just one tap.

---

## 1. Features and Functionalities

* **Continuous Monitoring (Buffer):** The app records in a temporary cycle (cache), ensuring you never miss an important moment.
* **Background Recording:** Works while you use other apps or with the screen turned off.
* **Retroactive Saving:** Choose to save the last 30s, 1min, 3min, 5min, or 10min.
* **Multiple Triggers:**
    * Main screen buttons.
    * Volume Buttons (**Premium**).
    * Camera Sensor Coverage (**Premium**).
    * Sound/Audio Detection (**Premium**).
    * Bluetooth media buttons (headphones, car controls).
    * Home screen Widget.
    * Quick Settings Tile.
* **Camera Customization:**
    * Multiple lens support: Main, Front, Ultra-wide, Telephoto, and external USB camera (OTG).
    * Video quality: SD, HD, FHD (Full HD), and UHD (4K).
    * Frame rate: 30 FPS or 60 FPS.
    * Capture format: 16:9 or 4:3.
* **Visual Elements (Watermark - Premium):**
    * Custom text.
    * Current date and time.
    * Current speed (via GPS).
    * Location coordinates (GPS).
* **PiP Mode (Picture-in-Picture - Premium):** Floating window to view the camera while using other apps.
* **Automation (Premium):**
    * Auto-start when connecting a charger or Bluetooth.
    * Auto-start upon opening the application.
* **Storage Management:**
    * Quota limit for cache (2GB, 5GB, or 10GB).
    * Automatic cleanup of temporary files.
* **Integrated Gallery:**
    * Management of saved clips.
    * Favorites and filters (Recent, Favorites, All).
    * Direct sharing with an option to remove audio.

---

## 2. Step-by-Step Tutorial

### Step 1: Initial Setup and Permissions
* When opening the app for the first time, grant the necessary permissions: **Camera, Microphone, Notifications, and Location** (required for speed/GPS watermark).
* **TIP:** Disable battery optimizations for CatchBack in your Android settings to prevent the system from closing the app in the background.

### Step 2: Quality and Camera Adjustments
* Tap the **Menu** icon (three bars) and go to **"Settings"**.
* In the **"Camera"** tab, select the desired lens and video quality.
* In the **"General"** tab, set the cache limit (temporary storage).

### Step 3: Start Monitoring
* On the main screen, tap the large circular button (**Play** icon).
* The status will change to **"Monitoring..."** and an activity timer will appear.
* You will see a persistent notification indicating that CatchBack is active.

### Step 4: Save a Moment
* When something interesting happens, you have several ways to save:
    1.  Open the app and tap one of the time cards (e.g., **"30 SEC"**).
    2.  Use the **Volume button** (if configured in Premium options).
    3.  Tap the **"SAVE"** button on the Widget or in the Notification.
* The app will process the video and save it permanently to your gallery.

### Step 5: View and Share
* Access the **"Gallery"** via the side menu or the library icon on the main screen.
* Tap a video to play, or press and hold to select and share/delete.

---

## 3. Summary: How to Get Started Now

1.  **OPEN** CatchBack and **ACCEPT** all permissions.
2.  **TAP** the **PLAY** button to start monitoring.
3.  **EXIT** the app and use your phone normally.
4.  **WHEN YOU WANT TO SAVE** something that just occurred, press the Volume button or use the Notification button.
5.  **DONE!** The video will be in the app's Gallery, ready to be shared.

> [!NOTE]
> **NOTE:** The "Watermark" and "4K Quality" features may increase video processing time after saving.
